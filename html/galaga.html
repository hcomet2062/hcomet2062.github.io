Greenfoot: Galaga

This Greenfoot game I created in September 2019 has the same premise as Galaga, a popular arcade game. However, I decided to randomly generate the enemies rather than have them fly at intricate patterns. Destroying 'bees' adds 50 pts, 'moths' add 20 pts, and 'ebullets' add 5 pts.

To play this game, the player must repeatedly press space bar for the bullets to come out of the ship. Holding the space bar will not result to anything. The ship can only move left or right, like the original Galaga game, and does so with the left and right arrow keys.

To win the game in the Invasion World, the player must be able to attain 10,000 pts. Since the video is too big to upload here, I have uploaded it to YouTube. You can view it here. Be sure to set the quality to the highest level to be able to see it clearly.

Given more time, I would like to be able to add sound to the game. Also, I would like to dismantle the score system in Level World since as of right now, score is shared to all the worlds. I would like to be able to animate the enemies as well, similar to the original Galaga game. Displaying the score and high score in the Invasion world may also be beneficial.
