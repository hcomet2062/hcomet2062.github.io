This is a Username Generator that I developed in August of 2018 during a summer camp.

This app contains 3 screens. The 1st and 2nd are shown in the slideshow but the 3rd is the end result which could be seen in either videos. The 3rd element in the slideshow demonstrates what happens when you type out the information as the last element in the slideshow displays the result of clicking random

Now that I think about it, this could suffice as a password generator because of its jumble of letters and numbers. However, to make it a stronger password generator, I would need to include capital letters and symbols.

As for the ideas that I had, I thought of making a home screen to introduce the app and background music to make this a bit more entertaining. However, creating either of these resulted to the app to crash because of the limited storage that Thunkable used so I could not implement any of these ideas.
